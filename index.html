What is WISTEM?

WISTEM is a school initiative that encourages students, especially girls, to explore Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) through hands-on projects, workshops, and events. It aims to make STEM accessible, fun, and inspiring for everyone.

About Us

We are a team of high school students passionate about STEM who created this club to combine our love for science with tools that make learning interactive and fun.

From podcasts to projects done as the WISTEM club of R.N. PODAR SCHOOL, our site is a way of sharing knowledge, inspiring curiosity, and encouraging others to explore the fascinating world of STEM.

As members and ambassadors of one of only two WISTEM clubs in India, we would love for you to be a part of this small step towards equitable education and learning beyond textbooks!
